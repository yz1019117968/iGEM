Problems

The world is facing 3 problems that affect our life expectancy.

1. Unavailability of medicines in developing countries due to low affordability. About 80% of Africans who are on or below the middle-income, rely on public health facilities. Nevertheless, due to the shortages of critical drugs in public health facilities, many patients die of easily curable diseases.

2. The number and types of human diseases have risen across the world. Due to global warming, temperature rises and bacteria becomes more active and leads to disease outbreaks.

3. Higher demands in the market for effective anti-microbial and anti-cancer drugs. However, the traditional drug discovery process is expensive, complicated and time-consuming…

Solutions

1. Natural products are sets of small molecules derived from the natural environment or any life forms that are not involved in primary metabolism. Fungi represents a major source of natural products.

2. About 40% of the drugs today are derived from natural products or based on natural product derivatives. The advantages are high molecular mass, great molecular rigidity, high hydrophilicity and complex structure.

3. Our project aims to generate a new class of genetically engineered variecolin bioactive compounds from the secondary metabolites of Aspergillus oryzae fungi which are potent antibacterial and immunostimulant drugs.
